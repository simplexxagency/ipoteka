About

Iryna founded Ipoteka Mortgage Inc. after spending 18 years with RBC as a top performing Mortgage Specialist.

Over those years Iryna was going through many different client scenarios and situations and was able to find the best possible solution for each and every client. Iryna’s creativity, customer service, timely and consistent communication made her the best mortgage broker in British Columbia, who helped thousands to achieve their dream to own their home.

When she has a spare time, Iryna loves to read, travel and play sport.
